Holiday Cheese Ball

Ingredients:

   1 package (8oz, 2 Cups) shredded sharp cheddar cheese
   1 package (3 0z) chream cheese
 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
   1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
   1 tablespoon pimento
   1 tablespoon chopped green bell pepper
   1 teaspoon paprika
 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
 1/2 cup Kellogg's All-Bran Original ceral


Directions:

1.  Let cheeses stand at room temperature to soften.  In a small electric mixer
bowl, beat cheeses until thoroghly blended and fluffy.

2.  Add onion, pimiento, green pepper, paprika and garlic salt.  Beat until well
combined.  Shape into a ball.

Roll in cereal.  Wrap and chill.  Before serving, let stand at room temperature
to soften slightly.  Serve with crackers.
