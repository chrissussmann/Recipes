Cheese Ball


 1 block cracker barrel extra sharp cheese, shredded
16 oz cream cheese
 1 tablespoon minced green onion
 1 tablespoon minced green pepper
 2 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
 1 small jar drained pimentos
 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
 1 minced jalapeno (optional)
   Salt and Pepper


Mix and and form into ball.  Roll into chopped nuts before serving.
