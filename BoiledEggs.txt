Perfectly Boiled Eggs

1 dozen eggs
water to cover
Pinch of salt

Place the eggs in a saucepan and cover with water.  Season with salt.  Place pan
over medium heat and bring to boil.  Cook for two minutes.  Remove from heat and
cover with lid.  Allow eggs to sit for 11 minutes.  Drain and cool eggs for two
minutes in ice water.  Drain and peel the eggs.
