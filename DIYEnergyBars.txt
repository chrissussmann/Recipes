Homemade Energy Bars

Makes 9 bars

6 ounces pitted dates (1 cup)
¼ cup sunflower seeds
¾ cup whole raw almonds
2 tablespoons chia seeds
¾ cup dried cranberries
2 tablespoons coconut oil

1. Line 9-inch square baking dish with parchment paper, leaving excess hanging
over the sides to make a sling.

2. Process all ingredients in food processor until finely ground, about 2
minutes. Transfer mixture to prepared baking dish spread into even layer,
pressing down using offset spatula or hand to firmly compress. Transfer to
refrigerator and chill until mixture is firm, about 20 minutes.

3. Lift parchment sling out of baking dish and place on cutting board. Using
sharp knife, cut into 9 bars. Bars can be refrigerated for up to 1 week or
frozen up to 1 month.
