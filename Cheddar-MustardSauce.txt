Cheddar-Mustard Cream Sauce

The combination of cheddar and mustard goes well with cauliflower.

 1 cup heavy cream
 4 oz shredded cheddar cheese (about a cup)
 1 tablespoon whole-grain mustard
   pinch of cayenne pepper

Place cream in small saucepan and bring to a bare simmer over medium heat.  
When the cream is steaming and just starting to bubble around the edge of 
pan, turn off the heat and add the cheese. mustard and cayenne.  Whisk 
together until smooth.  Season with salt and pepper to taste.
