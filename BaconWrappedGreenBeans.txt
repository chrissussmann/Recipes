
Green Bean Bundles
-----------------------------------
  3 cans whole green beans
 15 plus strips bacon cut in half
  ¾ cup melted butter
  ¾ cup brown sugar
  ½ tsp garlic salt
  1 T. liquid smoke
  Dash soy sauce

Mix butter, sugar, garlic salt, liquid smoke, and soy sauce in bowl. Set
aside.  Secure 9 to 10 beans in a bundle, use bacon to wrap around
beans. Secure with toothpick Fill casserole dish with bundles. Pour bowl
with other ingredients over top.  Refridgerate overnight. Bake for 30
minutes at 350 uncovered.

Bon Apetit!
