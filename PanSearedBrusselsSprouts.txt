Pan-Seared Brussels Sprouts with Cranberries & Pecans

Ingredients
 1 pound brussels sprouts, de-stemed and halved
 ? cup fresh cranberries
 ? cup gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
 ? cup pecans
 2 cups barley, cooked
 1 tablespoon maple syrup
 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
   Olive oil
   Salt & pepper

Instructions

Heat skillet with drizzling of olive oil over medium heat. Season brussels
sprouts with salt & pepper and combine with cranberries in skillet.  Cook
sprouts and cranberries for 8-10 minutes, until berries begin to burst and
sprouts become tender. Add balsamic vinegar and maple syrup. Stir to coat and
remove from heat.  Toss barley, sprouts, cranberries, and pecans in a large
bowl. Top with gorgonzola and serve.
