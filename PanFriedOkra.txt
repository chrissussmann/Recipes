Country Style Pan-fried Okra


Ingredients


4 Cups sliced okra
1/4 Cup Flour
1/8 Cup Cornmeal
1/4 Cup Canola Oil


Directions


Mix Okra with flour and cornmeal until completely coated. Pour oil in pan to heat. When oil is hot, add okra mixture to pan and stir til cooked.


Makes 4 1-cup servings.


Number of Servings: 4
