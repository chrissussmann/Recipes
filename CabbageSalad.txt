Cabbage Salad

1 small head of cabbage, chopped
5 green onions, chopped

Mix together in a large zip-loc bag for six to eight hours.  

Mix:
2/3 cup oil
1/2 cup white vinegar
  2 tablespoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
    Chicken Ramen Seasoning Packet

Just before serving toss cabbage with dressing.  And add:

3/4 cup toasted slivered almonds
