Taco Seasoning Mix
---------------------

   4 tablespoons chili powder
   1 teaspoon garlic powder
   1 teaspoon onion powder
 1/4 teaspoon ground red pepper
   1 teaspoon dried oregano
   2 teaspoon paprika
   6 teaspoons cumin
   4 teaspoons sea salt
   4 teaspoons black pepper

Put all ingredients into mason jar and shake to mix.  Approximately 3
tablespoons equals one store bought package.  Use 3 heaping tablespoons per
pound of meat.
