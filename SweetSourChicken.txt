Sweet and Sour Chicken

 1/4 cup oil
 8oz russian dressing
 8oz apricot preserves
   1 envelope onion soup mix
   1 teaspoon accent
   1 teaspoon salt
   4 boneless chicken breasts

Mix ingredients and pour over chicken breast in baking pan.  Cook covered for
about 30 minutes at 350.  Uncover and broil to your liking.
