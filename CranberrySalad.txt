Grandma's Cranberry Salad


Ingredients


1 cup cranberries
1 cup apples
Pulse in food processor till finely chopped. Add 3/4 cup of granulated
sugar and let sit in refrigerator for one hour. You may freeze at this
point or continue. 


Add 1 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
2/3 cup miniature marshmellows
Stir and serve!


Makes 8 servings. Recipe can be doubled or tripled easily.
