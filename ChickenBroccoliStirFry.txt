Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry

Mix ½ cup orange juice, ¼ cup soy, 1 Tbsp each sugar and garlic and 2
tsp cornstarch in a small bowl until blended.  Cut 1# chicken thighs and
toss with 2 Tbsp cornstarch in large ziplock.  Heat a tablespoon of oil
over medium-high heat.  Add chicken and stir fry for 5 minutes until
cooked and remove.  Heat 2 tsp oil in same skillet and fry 4 cups
broccoli 3 - 5 minutes until crisp-tender.  Add soy mixture to skillet
and bring to a simmer and cook a minute until sauce is thickened.  Toss
with chicken and serve.
